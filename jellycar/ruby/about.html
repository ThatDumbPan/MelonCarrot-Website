What IS JellyCar Ruby?

JellyCar Ruby is a fan-mod of JellyCar 3. JellyCar 3 was made
by Walaber and published by Disney.

The team:

Johark (Lead designer, Level design, Composer, Texture artist, Car design)

Pan_ (Original JC3 modder, Level design, Car design, Web design)
